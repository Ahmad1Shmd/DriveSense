We Provide Driver Safety & Health Monitoring Technology

DriveSense is an advanced AI-powered system designed to continuously monitor the driver's health and alertness. By using a camera and heart rate sensor, the system detects signs of fatigue, drowsiness, or abnormal heart rhythms, triggering timely alerts to ensure the driver’s safety on the road.
